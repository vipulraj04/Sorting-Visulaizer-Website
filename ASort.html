SORTING!!

Sorting Is A Method Of Arranging The Elements in a Particular Order Usually Ascending And Descending Order.

Why Is It Important:!

  • Make The Data Easier To Search and Analyze.

Applications!
  • Sorting Data In Excel.
  • Sorting Of Data By Price On E-Commerce Website.

Classification Of Sorting Algorithms:
  • Comparision Based:

    Compare Elements In Order To Determine Their Correct Position:

    Example:Bubble Sort and Quick Sort:

  • Non-Comparision Based:

    Do Not Relay on Element Comparision To Determine Their Correct Position:

    Example:Radix Sort and Bucket Sort:

  • Stable Sorting:

    Do Not Relay on Element Comparision To Determine Their Correct Position:

    Example:Radix Sort and Bucket Sort:

  • Unstable Sorting:

    Do Not Relay on Element Comparision To Determine Their Correct Position:

    Example:Radix Sort and Bucket Sort:

Different Types Of Sortings:
  • Bubble Sort
  • Selection Sort
  • Insertion Sort
  • Merge Sort
  • Quick Sort
  • Radix Sort
  • Bucket Sort

Time Complexity:  The Time Complexity is the Computational complexity that describes the amount of computer time it takes to run an Algorithm.
